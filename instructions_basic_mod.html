About this HIT:

  • Please only participate in this HIT if you have normal color vision.

  • It should take about 10 minutes.

  • You will take part in an experiment involving visual perception. You will be given 3 images - a reference image in the middle and two images on the sides. Your task it to determine which of the two images - left or right - looks closer to the one in the middle. Some of these may be obvious. Others may be more difficult. In those cases, give your best guess.

  • You will complete a short practice (less than 1 minute) before starting the main task.
